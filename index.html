Who are we?


Age of Elysian is a server that has been in business for nearly half a decade, and we've amassed quite a community during that time. Most player-suggested features have found their way to the server, and nowadays we are likely to be the most feature-packed cracked Slimefun-Towny server, with an up-to-date slimefun.

For the record, the server is entirely un-modded, you don't need any special client for it, although clients such as optifine are allowed and encouraged.

The server is cracked, so everyone is free to join. Unlike some other cracked servers, however, we host an experienced team of staff well aware of the risks or running an offline-mode server, and are more than capable with dealing with anything the script-kiddies have thrown at us so far.

If you're interested in joining, just type play.ageofelysian.top in your Multiplayer List and give it a go! If not, well, read along.

Discord!


We have a highly social (and somewhat meme-y) community on our Discord server. Here, give it a go: Discord. Even if you're not really into minecraft or are just taking a break, feel free to join and spend some time with us!

We also have a Minecraft-to-Discord integration bridge, so if you have a rank on the server or have linked your MC and Discord accounts with /discord link ingame, you can chat with the people on the server just by chatting in the #ingamechat channel.
